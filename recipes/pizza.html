Pizza

Description

Pizza is a classic Italian dish loved worldwide for its versatility and delicious flavors. The perfect pizza starts with a crisp yet tender dough, topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and a variety of toppings like fresh vegetables, savory meats, or herbs. It’s a meal that can be customized to suit any taste, making it an ideal dish for casual gatherings or family dinners. Whether you prefer the simplicity of a Margherita or the bold flavors of a loaded supreme pizza, making pizza at home is both fun and rewarding.

This recipe will guide you through making a homemade pizza with a perfectly chewy crust, flavorful sauce, and customizable toppings. Using fresh ingredients and a hot oven, you'll create a pizza that's fresher and tastier than anything from a pizzeria.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • ¾ cup warm water (100–110°F)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the Sauce

  • 1 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Toppings (optional but Chef Tony Advises that you do)

  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Sliced vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, mushrooms, onions)
  • Pepperoni or cooked sausage

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough
    • In a small bowl, mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until frothy.
    • In a large bowl, combine flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
    • Knead the dough on a floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
    • Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for 1–2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  2. Make the Sauce
    • In a small saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until slightly thickened.
  3. Assemble the Pizza
    • Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
    • Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a 12-inch circle. Transfer to a baking sheet or pizza peel.
    • Spread sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border. Sprinkle with cheese and add toppings of choice.
  4. Bake
    • Transfer the pizza to the oven and bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
    • Let the pizza cool slightly before slicing and serving.
  5. Eat the Pizza!!
